Who We Are

We are Carnegie Mellon University's undergraduate marine robotics team. We compete in RoboSub, an annual competition in which universities from around the world compete by designing autonomous underwater vehicles (AUVs) that must navigate obstacles, move and manipulate objects, detect hydroacoustic pingers, and fire torpedoes. Founded in 2018, this interdisciplinary group has competed in 2019 and 2022, where we recieved third place overall. We aim to compete in 2023, debuting our accomplishments on our new vehicle!
