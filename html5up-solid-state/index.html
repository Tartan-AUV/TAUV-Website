Engaged

Our efforts go beyond the workshop and classroom. Being students at Carnegie Mellon
University means we are always looking to shape and improve our community.

"We often engage with the local Pittsburgh community by volunteering with high school students on FIRST robotics teams near campus. We've enjoyed the rewarding experience of helping younger students get exposed to hands-on, fun engineering problems and it's a great way to extend our resources and what we've learned from our own robotics experience to other ambitious students."
~ Cole Herber | Mechanical Engineering

Albatross

Albatross is a compact research platform AUV with hot-swap-able parts intended for software and electronics rapid prototyping.

Kingfisher

Kingfisher is TAUV's flagship vehicle equipped with a complete sensor suite and set of actuated subsystems for competition use.
